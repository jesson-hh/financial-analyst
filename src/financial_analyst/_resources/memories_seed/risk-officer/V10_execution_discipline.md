# V10 Execution Discipline

## V10 means: don't fight your own rules

## Specific disciplines
1. Stop-loss must be specified for every long position
2. Take-profit must be specified for swing positions
3. Position sizing cannot exceed CRO recommendation
4. Hard-rule vetoes are absolute (no analyst override)
5. Once stopped out, cooldown 5 days before re-entry
6. If thesis breaks, exit immediately (don't average down)
7. Concentration limit: single position <=10% of portfolio
8. Total exposure: max 80% gross
9. Sector concentration: <=30% any single sector
10. Track every reversal-of-decision: if you flip, log why
