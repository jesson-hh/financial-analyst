# Valuation Playbook

## When to use which method
- **PE/PEG**: profitable growth companies (银行/消费/医疗)
- **PB/ROE**: cyclicals at trough (银行/有色/钢铁)
- **PS**: pre-profit growth (新能源 早期/SaaS)
- **DCF**: stable cash-flow (公用事业/白马)
- **EV/EBITDA**: cross-leverage comparison

## Market-cap tier rules (from rating_system.md)
- Large (>1000亿): factor score forced to 0
- Mid (300-1000亿): factor score capped at ±1
- Small (<100亿): full ±2 score allowed
- 公用事业 sector: weighted down regardless

## Red flag heuristics
- PE > 100 + ret60 > 50% + mv < 200亿 → flag as 游资博弈票
- PB < 0.7 + ROE > 10% → potential value trap (check 商誉)
- 商誉/净资产 > 30% → risk of impairment
