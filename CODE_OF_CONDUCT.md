# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct / 贡献者公约行为准则

> 中英文都接受 (English / 中文 both accepted).
> Project: `financial-analyst` ([觀瀾](https://github.com/jesson-hh/financial-analyst))

## Our Pledge / 承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

我们承诺让本社区的参与对所有人都是无骚扰的体验, 不分年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、族裔、性征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育、社经地位、国籍、外貌、种族、种姓、肤色、宗教、性取向. 我们承诺以促进开放、包容、健康的方式互动.

## Our Standards / 标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment / 鼓励的行为:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people / 对他人富有同理心
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences / 尊重不同观点与经验
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback / 给出与接受建设性反馈
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience / 承担责任、致歉、并从中学习
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community / 关注社区整体利益

Examples of unacceptable behavior / 不可接受的行为:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind / 性化语言或图像、任何形式的性关注
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks / 挑衅、侮辱、人身或政治攻击
- Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission / 未经允许公开他人隐私
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting / 在专业场合明显不当的其它行为

**A-share 投资讨论特别说明 / A-share investing discussions**: 本项目是研究工具, 不是荐股社区. Issue / PR / discussion 里**不接受**「拉人接盘」「带节奏」「人身攻击其它投资观点」等行为. 技术分歧请用数据和回测说话, 不用情绪.

## Enforcement Responsibilities / 执行责任

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

项目维护者有权且有责任删除、编辑、拒绝不符合本准则的评论、提交、代码、wiki 编辑、issue 等贡献.

## Scope / 适用范围

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (GitHub repo, issues, PRs, discussions, social media accounts representing the project), and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

本准则适用于所有社区空间 (GitHub 仓库、issue、PR、讨论、代表项目的社交账号), 以及个人公开代表社区时.

## Enforcement Guidelines / 执行指南

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. Correction / 纠正
**Community Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.
**Consequence**: A private, written warning, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning / 警告
**Community Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.
**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time.

### 3. Temporary Ban / 临时封禁
**Community Impact**: A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.
**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time.

### 4. Permanent Ban / 永久封禁
**Community Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.
**Consequence**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
