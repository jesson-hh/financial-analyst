# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct / 贡献者公约

## Our Pledge / 承诺

**EN**: We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

**中文**: 我们作为成员、贡献者和领导者承诺, 让所有人都能在社区中获得无骚扰体验, 无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、族裔、性别特征、性别认同和表达、经验水平、教育、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向.

## Our Standards / 行为准则

### Positive behavior / 鼓励的行为

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people / 对他人表现出同理心与友善
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences / 尊重不同意见 / 立场 / 经验
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback / 给出 / 优雅接受建设性反馈
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for our mistakes / 为错误承担责任并道歉
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community / 关注整体社区利益

### Unacceptable behavior / 不可接受的行为

- The use of sexualized language or imagery / 使用性化语言或图像
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks / 嘲讽、侮辱、人身或政治攻击
- Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission / 未经允许公开他人隐私
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate / 其它不当行为

## Scope / 适用范围

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (GitHub, Discord, WeChat groups, in-person events), and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

本公约适用于所有社区场所 (GitHub / Discord / 微信群 / 线下活动), 以及成员代表社区出席公共场合时.

## Enforcement / 执行

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at **xuyi1030@proton.me**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

如有举报, 邮件至 **xuyi1030@proton.me**. 所有投诉将得到公正及时的处理.
