# Analyst Playbook (V1-V10)

## V1: Industry tailwind > company quality
Bet on rising tide; even mediocre companies float.

## V2: Margin trend > absolute margin
Improving 毛利率 from 30→35% is stronger than steady 40%.

## V3: Revenue mix shift
Look for high-margin product replacing low-margin one.

## V4: Four modes (立讯/华工/仕佳/信维)
- 立讯模式: big-client lock-in (Apple)
- 华工模式: domestic substitution
- 仕佳模式: tech leap (CPO etc)
- 信维模式: capacity expansion paying off

## V5: Capacity utilization > capacity itself
Idle factories aren't assets.

## V6: Order visibility (在手订单 vs 在产订单)

## V7: 单价 vs 销量 — which is driving growth

## V8: Inventory cycle
高库存 + 价格降 = 杀估值

## V9: Catalyst calendar
Upcoming events that could re-rate

## V10: Execution discipline
- Stop-loss specified
- Position sizing capped
- Hard rules cannot be overridden
- Concentration limits enforced

## Usage
Every Bull/Bear bullet should cite at least one V-anchor.
